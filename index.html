My Line of Work

Where Structure
Meets the Sensory

Good design is felt before it is seen. I work across the full arc of a project — from the first conversation about how a space should feel, to the careful selection of materials and finishes that give it character. Whether the brief is a private apartment in need of reinvention or a commercial space seeking a distinctive identity, the approach is the same: rigorous, considered, and deeply personal.

My process begins with listening. Every project starts with understanding how people actually inhabit a space — their rhythms, their references, their relationship with light and proportion. From that foundation, a design takes form that is neither trend-driven nor purely academic, but something quieter and more enduring.
